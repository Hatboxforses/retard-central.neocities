Jazz Jackrabbit was a game series developed and published by Epic MegaGames in August 1st 1994 that
featured Jazz Jackrabbit an anthropomorphic hare that goes up against a turtle by the name of "Devan
Shell" that kidnaps princess Eva Earlong. The 2nd game follows this story up with Jazz and his siblings
Spaz & Lori trying to get Eva's stolen wedding ring. Unfortanately the 3rd game never got a release
because of the inability to find a publisher. The 3rd game was actually meant to be a third-person
shooter with platforming and adventure game elements" with the mouse being used to aim & the
keyboard being used to move as you could probably guess the game was made to be 3d with the
alternate title Jazz Jackrabbit 3D: Adventures of a Mean Green Hare & the inclusion of the mouse to the
control scheme. There were no games released after the 3rd game's cancellation & only Epic Games
knows why (probably Fortnite).
